## How we help you

Every day the Integration Team works hard to support our merchants. Due to the complexity of ecommerce platforms, it can be quite a challenge! 

There is always the chance of a conflict with third-party plugins, customization, or server configurations. We always test our ecommerce integrations extensively before release, and ensure the situation is resolved.

Sometimes technical issues fall outside our scope, but we will always try to support and assist you to the best of our ability.

## How you can help us

We stand by our service and need your help to spread the word. Please consider writing an honest review on a platform of your choice.  

We would greatly appreciate it!
